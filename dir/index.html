What will you learn in Graphic Design Course?

MODULE 1

Graphic Design Foundation

  • Overview of Graphic Design: Understanding the role and importance of graphic design in various industries.
  • Design Principles: Basic elements and principles of design (color theory, typography, layout, etc.).
MODULE 2

Design Software and Tools

  • Adobe Photoshop : Photo editing and compositing.
  • Adobe Illustrator : Vector graphic creation.
  • Adobe InDesign: Layout design for brochures, magazines, and more.
MODULE 3

Design Thinking and Concept Development

  • Creative Process: From brainstorming to final design.
  • Client Briefs and Design Solutions: Meeting client needs and communication through design.
MODULE 4

Branding and Identity Design

  • Brand Identity: Creating logos, color schemes, and brand guidelines.
  • Marketing Materials Design: Designing business cards, flyers, and promotional items.
MODULE 5

Digital Design

  • Web and Mobile Design Basics: UX/UI principles, responsive design.
  • Social Media and Content Creation: Designing for engagement on digital platforms.
MODULE 6

Portfolio Development

  • Project Selection: Choosing your best work for display.
  • Digital Portfolio Creation: Building an online portfolio.
  • Feedback and Revision: Learning from critique to improve your designs.
MODULE 7

Career and Industry Insight

  • Graphic Design Careers: Exploring job roles and paths in the industry.
  • Freelancing and Entrepreneurship: Starting your own design business.
  • Networking and Community: Engaging with the design community for opportunities and growth.
MODULE 8

Final Project

  • Comprehensive Design Project: Applying skills to a real-world scenario.
  • Presentation and Review: Showcasing your project to peers and instructors for feedback.
